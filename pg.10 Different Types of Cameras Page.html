Types of Cameras for Photography

Compact Digital Camera



Bestbuy.com

Compact digital cameras are a breeze for beginning photographers who are just starting out trying to familarize themselves with cameras. It it very user friendly and does not require you to adjust settings every time in order to produce a quality photo. It it also known for being light and compact so you can place it in your pocket and take it out when you need it!

Main Points

  • Beginner Friendly
  • Lighter than most cameras
  • Affordable
  • Not a lot of settings to adjust

Digital SLR Cameras



bestbuy.com

Digital SLR - Digital Single Lens Reflex. This camera delivers spectacular photos because of the many settings and sensors. This camera also features interchangable lenses and has manual settings that you can change such as exposure and shutting speeds. Many photographers use this camera to take images that end up in various print materials such as magazines or head photo shots for portfolios.

Main Points

  • HD Output
  • Higher Cost
  • Settings can be customized
  • Heavier/Bulky

Mirrorless Cameras



bestbuy.com

Mirrorless cameras are similar to Digital SLR Cameras but instead they do not have the mirror inside where the light can bounce off of the sensor. These cameras like the Digital SLR can also produce high quality HD images and videos. They are also much lighter than their counterparts.

Main Points

  • Fast shutter speed
  • Short Battery Life
  • Lighter than most cameras
  • Great for videos

Action Cameras



amazon.com

Action cameras have become very popular since the Go-Pro and they are rising in popularity. Action cameras can capture great angles and can be mounted so on helments to capture the action firsthand. They are rugged, waterproof, and can be viewed remotely via your smart phone.

Main Points

  • Versatile
  • Remote View
  • Limited Zoom
  • Compact

360 Cameras



bestbuy.com

360 Degree Cameras are similar to action cameras only they produce panoramic pictures. These cameras are also great for live streaming and the updated ones even have trackers in them so they move and follow you as you walk around.

Main Points

  • Realistic 360 photos
  • Versatile
  • Great to use when streaming
  • Limited focus

Film Cameras



bestbuy.com

Film cameras were used before digital cameras came out. Like the name these cameras take film unlike their counterparts that read to an SD card. These cameras, though outdated produce great photos that have a vintage look to them.

Main Points

  • Outdated
  • Cheap lenses
  • Analog Settings
  • Gives photos a vintage look
